💼 Placement Policy

1. Eligibility for Placements:
Students must have a minimum CGPA of 6.5 to register for campus placements.
Students with more than 2 active backlogs are not allowed to sit for pre-placement talks or interviews.

2. Internship Rules:
A mandatory 8-week summer internship must be completed at the end of the third academic year.
Internships must be approved by the placement cell prior to commencement.

3. Placement Process:
Once a student secures a job offer from a company during campus placements, they are immediately deregistered from the placement process (One Student, One Job policy).
Exceptions are made only if the subsequent company offers a compensation package that is at least 1.5x higher than the secured offer (Dream Company status).
