TITLE: CIT Placement Process and Rules

SECTION: Placement Cell Overview
Institution: Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT)

Placement Cell Role:
- Directed by a Professor-ranked staff member
- Responsible for securing placements for graduating students
- Maintains partnerships with national and multinational companies
- Facilitates:
  - Pre-placement talks
  - Campus interviews
  - Recruitment drives

Student Involvement:
- Student representatives from each department coordinate placement activities

Meetings:
- Monthly review meetings for planning
- General body meetings for final-year students at the beginning of academic year

SECTION: Placement Process
Step 1: Pre-Placement Talk (PPT)
- Company introduction, roles, salary, work culture

Step 2: Written Test / Online Assessment
- Aptitude, technical tests, coding rounds

Step 3: Group Discussion (GD)
- Communication, teamwork, analytical skills

Step 4: Technical Interview
- Domain knowledge and problem solving

Step 5: HR Interview
- Personality, communication, cultural fit

Step 6: Offer Letter
- Final selection and job offer

Special Note:
- Companies compete to be "Day One" recruiters

Keywords: placement process, PPT, GD, interview rounds

SECTION: Placement Rules and Policies
- Registration with Placement Cell is mandatory
- Formal dress code required
- Carry ID card and resume
- Attendance in all placement activities is compulsory
- Absence without notice may lead to debarment
- Must meet academic and attendance requirements
- Industrial training must be completed
