TITLE: Code of Conduct - Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT)
INSTITUTION: Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT), Coimbatore

================================================================================
SECTION: IDENTITY AND CAMPUS ACCESS
================================================================================
Rules:
- Students must carry institution-issued ID cards at all times
- ID must be shown when requested by faculty, staff, or security

Loss Handling:
- Lost ID cards must be reported immediately
- Replacement must be done as per institutional protocol

Keywords: ID card rules CIT, campus access CIT, student identity

================================================================================
SECTION: DRESS CODE
================================================================================

Boys:
- Formal pants and shirts required
- Formal shoes mandatory
- Jeans are strictly prohibited during college hours

Girls:
- Churidar with long top and dupatta mandatory

General Rule:
- Students must maintain neat and formal appearance

Keywords: dress code CIT, uniform rules CIT, student appearance

================================================================================
SECTION: CLASS HOURS AND SCHEDULE
================================================================================

Class Timings:
- I Year: 8:30 AM – 4:00 PM
- II to Final Year: 9:00 AM – 4:45 PM
- Part-Time B.E: 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM

Attendance Rules:
- Late entry or early exit requires approval from class advisor/faculty

Keywords: class timings CIT, schedule CIT, attendance rules

================================================================================
SECTION: BREAK TIMINGS
================================================================================

I Year:
- Morning Break: 10:20 AM – 10:35 AM
- Lunch Break: 12:25 PM – 1:30 PM

II to Final Year:
- Morning Break: 10:50 AM – 11:05 AM
- Lunch Break: 12:55 PM – 2:00 PM

Keywords: break timings CIT, lunch break CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: ACADEMIC INTEGRITY
================================================================================

Rules:
- Cheating and malpractice are strictly prohibited
- Plagiarism is not allowed
- Copying assignments, lab records, or projects is forbidden
- Use of unauthorized materials during exams is prohibited

Consequences:
- Disciplinary action including suspension or expulsion

Keywords: academic integrity CIT, cheating rules, exam malpractice

================================================================================
SECTION: PROHIBITED CONDUCT
================================================================================

Strictly Forbidden:
- Drugs, alcohol, tobacco, intoxicants on campus
- Ragging (punishable by law under UGC regulations)
- Cyber-bullying, email theft, sharing private communications
- Posting harmful or offensive content on social media
- Bullying, harassment, discrimination
- Physical or verbal abuse
- Sexual harassment (handled under POSH policy)

Keywords: prohibited activities CIT, ragging rules CIT, harassment policy

================================================================================
SECTION: CAMPUS MOVEMENT
================================================================================

Rules:
- Loitering during class hours is prohibited
- Students must remain in classrooms/labs during scheduled hours
- Leaving campus requires permission from class advisor

Keywords: campus rules CIT, movement restrictions, attendance location

================================================================================
SECTION: TRANSPORTATION AND PARKING
================================================================================

Rules:
- Park vehicles only in designated areas
- Unauthorized parking leads to fines/towing
- Helmets mandatory for riders
- Maximum 2 riders per bike
- Follow campus traffic rules

Keywords: parking rules CIT, transport rules, helmet rule CIT

Keywords: library rules CIT, library fines, book handling

================================================================================
SECTION: ANTI-RAGGING POLICY
================================================================================

Legal Status:
- Ragging is a criminal offense under:
  - UGC Regulations
  - Tamil Nadu Prohibition of Ragging Act

Committee:
- Anti-Ragging Committee chaired by:
  - Dr. A. Rajeswari (Principal)
- Members include:
  - Police officer
  - Legal advisor
  - Faculty members
  - Student representatives

Enforcement:
- Anti-Ragging Squad conducts regular inspections:
  - Hostels
  - Canteens
  - Common areas

Reporting:
- Helpline: +91 94883 16334
- Email: principal.citoffice@cit.edu.in

Punishment:
- Immediate expulsion
- Criminal prosecution

Keywords: anti ragging CIT, ragging punishment, UGC rules CIT
