Tuition Fees and Fee Structure - Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT), Coimbatore
Academic Year 2026-27

1. B.E./B.Tech (Government Aided - Through TNEA Counselling):
I Year: Rs. 33,464
I Year Lateral Entry: Rs. 33,350
II Year: Rs. 31,735
III Year: Rs. 31,735
IV Year: Rs. 35,035
These are for government-aided seats filled through Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) counselling.

2. B.E./B.Tech (Self-Financed - Through Counselling):
I Year: Rs. 56,000 (Term I) + Rs. 36,000 (Term II) = Rs. 92,000 total
II Year: Rs. 54,585 (Term I) + Rs. 36,390 (Term II) = Rs. 90,975 total
III Year: Rs. 54,585 (Term I) + Rs. 36,390 (Term II) = Rs. 90,975 total
IV Year: Rs. 50,205 (Term I) + Rs. 33,470 (Term II) = Rs. 83,675 total
Self-financed seats are for management quota and other non-government counselling admissions.

3. B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (Un-Aided):
I Year: Rs. 53,000 (Term I) + Rs. 34,000 (Term II) = Rs. 87,000 total
II Year: Rs. 51,585 (Term I) + Rs. 34,390 (Term II) = Rs. 85,975 total
III Year: Rs. 51,585 (Term I) + Rs. 34,390 (Term II) = Rs. 85,975 total
IV Year: Rs. 47,205 (Term I) + Rs. 31,470 (Term II) = Rs. 78,675 total

4. MCA (Master of Computer Applications):
I Year: Rs. 59,360 (Term I) + Rs. 38,240 (Term II) = Rs. 97,600 total
II Year: Rs. 59,163 (Term I) + Rs. 39,442 (Term II) = Rs. 98,605 total

5. MBA (Master of Business Administration):
Semester I: Rs. 1,08,900
Semester II: Rs. 1,02,300
Semester III: Rs. 1,05,815
Semester IV: Rs. 1,02,300
Total MBA Programme Fee: Approximately Rs. 4,19,315

6. M.Sc. Programmes (Software Systems, Data Science, DCS, AIML):
Fees range from Rs. 77,300 to Rs. 86,060 per semester across 10 semesters (5-year integrated programme).

7. M.E. (Master of Engineering):
Semester fees range from Rs. 27,600 to Rs. 40,250 depending on the specialization and semester.

8. PTBE (Part-Time B.E.):
Semester fees range from Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 17,900.

9. Fee Payment Guidelines:
Fees must be paid at the beginning of each semester/term as per the academic calendar.
Late payment of fees may attract a penalty.
Students who fail to pay fees within the stipulated time may not be allowed to attend classes or appear for examinations.
Fee receipts must be preserved for future reference.

10. Fee Concessions:
Full tuition fee concession is available for SC/ST students and converts to Christianity for government-aided courses, regardless of family income.
BC/MBC students with family income below the prescribed limit may be eligible for partial fee concessions.
Students receiving government scholarships may have their fees adjusted accordingly.

11. Refund Policy:
Fee refunds for students withdrawing from the programme are processed as per the norms of Anna University and AICTE.
Refund requests must be submitted within the specified timeframe with proper documentation.

12. Additional Charges:
Hostel fees: Rs. 28,000 to Rs. 30,000 per annum (separate from tuition fees).
Examination fees are charged separately as per the Controller of Examinations' notification.
Laboratory fees, library fees, and other miscellaneous charges may apply as specified during admission.
