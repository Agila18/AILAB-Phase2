TITLE: Course Registration System - Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT)
INSTITUTION: Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT), Coimbatore

================================================================================
SECTION: CBCS SYSTEM OVERVIEW
================================================================================

System:
- Choice Based Credit System (CBCS)

Introduced:
- Credit System: 2001
- CBCS Implementation: 2015

Purpose:
- Provides flexibility in course selection
- Allows students to choose electives based on interests and career goals

Keywords: CBCS CIT, credit system CIT, flexible learning CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: COURSE CATEGORIES
================================================================================

1. Core Courses:
- Mandatory courses for each department/programme
- Form the foundation of the degree

2. Professional Electives:
- Department-specific electives
- Allow specialization in chosen domain

3. Open Electives:
- Courses from other departments
- Broaden interdisciplinary knowledge

4. Mandatory Courses:
- Non-credit courses
- Examples:
  - Science of Creativity and Professional Ethics (since 1987)
  - Environmental Science
  - Constitution of India

5. Laboratory Courses:
- Practical courses linked with theory subjects

6. Project Courses:
- Mini Projects: 2 credits (V and VI semesters)
- Final Year Projects

Keywords: course types CIT, electives CIT, core subjects CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: COURSE REGISTRATION PROCESS
================================================================================

Steps:
- Register at the beginning of each semester
- Registration period defined by academic office
- Consult faculty advisor before selecting courses
- Complete registration within first two weeks of semester

Support:
- Academic office assistance
- Faculty advisor guidance

Keywords: course registration CIT, semester registration process

================================================================================
SECTION: ADD/DROP PERIOD
================================================================================

Duration:
- First two weeks of semester

Rules:
- Students can add or drop elective courses without penalty
- After this period, changes are not allowed

Exception:
- Only with special approval from Head of Department (HOD)

Keywords: add drop CIT, course change rules CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: CREDIT LOAD
================================================================================

Typical Load:
- 20 to 25 credits per semester

Rules:
- Cannot exceed maximum credit limit without permission
- Extra courses require:
  - Academic advisor approval
  - HOD approval

Keywords: credit limit CIT, semester credits CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: ELECTIVE COURSE SELECTION
================================================================================

Features:
- Electives introduced based on industry needs
- Regular updates to match latest technology trends

Special Options:
- German or French as alternative to English (2nd semester)

Governance:
- Board of Studies reviews and updates electives

Keywords: elective selection CIT, foreign language option CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: INDUSTRIAL TRAINING
================================================================================

Requirement:
- Mandatory for all students

Duration:
- 4 weeks

Since:
- 2009

Evaluation:
- Training report
- Viva voce

Credits:
- Included in academic credit system

Condition:
- Must be completed to obtain degree

Keywords: industrial training CIT, internship requirement CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: MINI PROJECT
================================================================================

Details:
- Offered in V and VI semesters
- Credits: 2

Requirements:
- Register for mini project
- Select faculty guide

Purpose:
- Apply theoretical knowledge to practical problems

Keywords: mini project CIT, project credits CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: COURSE WITHDRAWAL
================================================================================

Option:
- Apply for 'W' (Withdrawal grade)

Conditions:
- Apply before mid-semester deadline
- Maximum 2 withdrawals per semester

Impact:
- No grade penalty
- Course must be completed later

Keywords: withdrawal CIT, W grade CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: BACKLOG / ARREAR COURSES
================================================================================

Rules:
- Failed courses must be re-registered
- Must clear arrears to complete degree

Advice:
- Prioritize clearing arrears before taking extra electives

Keywords: arrear rules CIT, backlog courses CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: SYLLABUS REVISION
================================================================================

Frequency:
- Every 4 years or as required

Responsible Bodies:
- Curriculum Development Cell
- Board of Studies

Purpose:
- Keep curriculum updated with industry trends

Keywords: syllabus update CIT, curriculum revision CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: PROGRAMME DURATION
================================================================================

Undergraduate:
- B.E / B.Tech: 4 years (8 semesters)

Postgraduate:
- M.E / M.Tech: 2 years (4 semesters)
- MCA: 2 years (4 semesters)
- MBA: 2 years (4 semesters)

Integrated:
- M.Sc: 5 years (10 semesters)

Doctoral:
- Ph.D: Minimum 3 years

Keywords: course duration CIT, semesters CIT, program length CIT
