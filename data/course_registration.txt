🎓 Course Registration

1. Add/Drop Courses:
The Add/Drop period is strictly active during the first two weeks of the semester.
Students can drop an elective course without any academic penalty during this window.
Any course drop after week two will reflect as a 'W' (Withdrawn) grade on the official transcript.

2. Credit Limits:
A full-time undergraduate student must register for a minimum of 15 credits and a maximum of 24 credits per semester.
Overloading credits beyond 24 is only permitted for seniors (4th-year students) with a minimum CGPA of 8.0, and requires approval from the Dean of Academic Affairs.
