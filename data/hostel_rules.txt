🏠 Hostel Rules

1. Timings:
The hostel gates strictly close at 10:00 PM on weekdays and 11:00 PM on weekends.
Students must use their biometric access to enter or exit the premises.

2. Mess Rules:
The dining hall operates during predefined hours: Breakfast (7 AM - 9 AM), Lunch (12 PM - 2 PM), and Dinner (7:30 PM - 9:30 PM).
Wasting food is a punishable offense and repeated offenders will face fines.
Outside food is not allowed inside the main dining hall.

3. Discipline:
No loud music or noise is permitted after 11:00 PM (Quiet Hours).
Students are fully responsible for the furniture and fixtures provided in their rooms. Any damages will be charged directly to the occupants.
