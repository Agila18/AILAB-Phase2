📊 Grading System

1. Grade Ranges:
The university follows a 10-point absolute grading scale.
O (Outstanding) : 90-100 marks (10 grade points)
A+ (Excellent) : 80-89 marks (9 grade points)
A (Very Good) : 70-79 marks (8 grade points)
B+ (Good) : 60-69 marks (7 grade points)
B (Average) : 50-59 marks (6 grade points)
C (Pass) : 40-49 marks (5 grade points)
F (Fail) : Below 40 marks (0 grade points)

2. CGPA Calculation:
The Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA) is calculated by dividing the total credit points earned by the total credits registered in that semester.
The Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is the weighted average of all SGPA earned across all completed semesters.
A minimum CGPA of 5.0 is required for graduation.
