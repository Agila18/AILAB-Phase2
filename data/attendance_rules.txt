TITLE: Attendance and Hostel Regulations
INSTITUTION: Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT), Coimbatore

SECTION: Overview
- This document outlines the academic attendance requirements and hostel regulations for students.
- The policies are designed to ensure discipline, safety, academic consistency, and responsible student behavior.

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Academic Attendance Policy

MINIMUM REQUIREMENT:
- Students must maintain at least 75% attendance in each course.
- Attendance is mandatory for:
  - Theory classes
  - Laboratory sessions
  - Practical and workshop classes

IMPORTANCE:
- Ensures continuous learning
- Helps in internal assessment performance
- Maintains academic discipline

EXAM ELIGIBILITY:
- Students below the required attendance may not be permitted to appear for End Semester Examinations.

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Attendance Monitoring System

- Attendance is recorded daily by faculty members.
- Attendance records are:
  - Digitally maintained
  - Periodically reviewed

WARNING SYSTEM:
- Students with low attendance may receive:
  - Warning notifications
  - Academic counseling

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Hostel Overview

- CIT provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls.
- Hostels are supervised by:
  - Wardens
  - Residential Tutors
- The hostel environment focuses on:
  - Safety
  - Discipline
  - Academic support

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Daily Hostel Attendance

- Hostel attendance is taken every day.
- Students must be present in the hostel during designated hours.

TIMINGS:
- Entry and exit timings are strictly followed.
- Late entry is monitored and recorded.

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Leave and Permission Rules

PRIOR PERMISSION:
- Students must obtain permission before:
  - Leaving hostel premises
  - Staying outside overnight
  - Returning late

APPROVAL AUTHORITIES:
- Residential Tutor
- Hostel Warden

PROCESS:
1. Submit leave request
2. Provide valid reason
3. Get approval before leaving

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Unauthorized Absence

- If a student is absent without permission:
  - Parents/guardians are informed immediately
  - The incident is recorded

REPEATED VIOLATIONS:
- May lead to:
  - Warning notices
  - Restriction of hostel privileges
  - Disciplinary committee action

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Late Entry Guidelines

- Students returning late must:
  - Inform the Residential Tutor in advance
  - Provide a valid reason

UNAPPROVED LATE ENTRY:
- Considered a rule violation
- May result in disciplinary action

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Safety and Security Measures

- Hostels are equipped with:
  - Controlled entry/exit points
  - Security personnel
  - Visitor monitoring system

VISITOR RULES:
- Visitors allowed only during permitted hours
- Entry must be recorded

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Student Responsibilities

- Maintain discipline inside hostel
- Follow timing regulations
- Respect wardens and staff
- Avoid unauthorized absence

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Disciplinary Actions

Minor Violations:
- Warning issued
- Counseling

Moderate Violations:
- Parent notification
- Restriction of movement

Major Violations:
- Hostel suspension
- Institutional disciplinary action

--------------------------------------------------

SECTION: Integration with Academic System

- Hostel discipline supports academic performance.
- Regular attendance in hostel contributes to:
  - Better study habits
  - Improved academic outcomes
