🏫 Attendance Rules

1. Minimum Attendance:
The university mandates an absolute minimum attendance of 75% in every individual course to be eligible to appear for the End Semester Examination.

2. Consequences of Shortfall:
Students with attendance between 65% and 75% may be allowed to sit for exams only upon paying a condonation fee and submitting valid medical documentation.
Students with attendance strictly below 65% will receive an 'FA' (Fail due to Attendance) grade and must repeat the course entirely in the next available semester.

3. Official Leave:
Students participating in university-approved hackathons, sports events, or cultural fests will receive an attendance waiver not exceeding 10% of total classes.
