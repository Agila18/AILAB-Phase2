TITLE: Examination Policy - Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT)
INSTITUTION: Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT), Coimbatore

================================================================================
SECTION: EXAMINATION STRUCTURE
================================================================================

Types of Examinations:
- Two Mid-term Examinations (Internal Assessments)
- One End Semester Examination (Term-End)

Evaluation Method:
- Final result = Internal Assessment + End Semester Exam

Keywords: exam structure CIT, internal assessment CIT, end semester exam

================================================================================
SECTION: EXAMINATION SCHEDULE
================================================================================

Academic Calendar:
- Odd Semester:
  - Duration: August – December
  - Exams: November – December

- Even Semester:
  - Duration: January – May
  - Exams: April – May

Timetable:
- Published on official CIT website before exams

Keywords: exam schedule CIT, semester exam dates CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: QUESTION PAPER SETTING
================================================================================

UG Programmes:
- Question papers set by:
  - Internal examiners
  - External examiners

PG Programmes:
- Fully autonomous system
- Question papers set internally by the institution

Purpose:
- Ensure fairness and quality

Keywords: question paper setting CIT, UG PG exams CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: VALUATION PROCESS
================================================================================

UG Evaluation:
- Answer scripts evaluated by:
  - Internal examiners
  - External examiners

Transparency:
- Students can view evaluated answer scripts

Revaluation Option:
- Students may apply if not satisfied

Keywords: valuation CIT, answer sheet review CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: PASSING CRITERIA
================================================================================

Requirements:
- Minimum marks in:
  - Internal assessment
  - End semester exam (separately)

Final Grade:
- Based on combined performance

Keywords: passing marks CIT, exam pass criteria

================================================================================
SECTION: REVALUATION PROCESS
================================================================================

Eligibility:
- Students not satisfied with results

Conditions:
- Apply within deadline set by Controller of Examinations
- Pay prescribed revaluation fee

Scope:
- Applicable for end-semester answer scripts

Keywords: revaluation CIT, exam rechecking CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: SUPPLEMENTARY EXAMINATIONS
================================================================================

Eligibility:
- Final year (outgoing) students with arrears

Purpose:
- Clear pending subjects before graduation

Additional Rule:
- Other students can write arrear exams in later semesters

Keywords: supplementary exam CIT, arrear exam CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: CONTINUOUS INTERNAL ASSESSMENT
================================================================================

Components:
- Mid-term tests
- Assignments
- Quizzes
- Seminars
- Lab performance

Special Note:
- Continuous lab/workshop/drawing assessment since 2001

Contribution:
- Internal marks combined with end semester marks

Keywords: internal marks CIT, continuous assessment CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: RESULT DECLARATION
================================================================================

Timeline:
- Results declared within 30–35 days after exams

Result Months:
- Odd Semester: January
- Even Semester: June

Mode:
- Published on official CIT website

Keywords: result date CIT, exam results CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: EXAMINATION MISCONDUCT
================================================================================

Prohibited Actions:
- Copying
- Use of unauthorized materials
- Impersonation

Penalties:
- Cancellation of exam
- Suspension
- Expulsion (based on severity)

Keywords: exam malpractice CIT, cheating rules CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: ELIGIBILITY FOR EXAMINATION
================================================================================

Requirements:
- Minimum 75% attendance in each course
- Completion of internal assessments
- Submission of assignments
- Payment of all fees and fines

Keywords: exam eligibility CIT, attendance requirement CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: MINI PROJECT EVALUATION
================================================================================

Details:
- Offered in V and VI semesters
- Credits: 2

Evaluation:
- Presentation
- Report submission
- Viva voce

Examiners:
- Internal and external evaluators

Keywords: mini project evaluation CIT, project assessment CIT

GLOBAL KEYWORDS
================================================================================

- CIT revaluation and arrear exams
- CIT exam eligibility rules
