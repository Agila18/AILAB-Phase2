📄 Exam Policy

1. Exam Types:
The university conducts two main types of examinations per semester: Midterm Examinations and End Semester Examinations.
Midterms are usually held in the 8th week of the semester, while End Semester Exams are held in the 16th and 17th weeks.

2. Passing Criteria:
A student must secure a minimum of 40% marks overall across continuous evaluations, midterms, and end-semester exams to pass a course.
Additionally, passing the end-semester exam separately with a minimum of 35% is mandatory.

3. Revaluation Rules:
Students who are not satisfied with their grades can apply for revaluation within 7 days of the result declaration.
A non-refundable fee of $50 per subject applies for revaluation requests.
Only End Semester Exam answer scripts are eligible for revaluation.
