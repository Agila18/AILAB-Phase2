Admission Requirements - Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT), Coimbatore

1. B.E. and B.Tech Programmes:

a. Minimum Educational Qualification:
A pass in HSC (Academic) with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry as subjects, OR
HSC Vocational Stream with engineering subjects plus related subjects, OR
Diploma in one of the Engineering Courses (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, or Electronics), OR
Any equivalent qualification approved by the Tamil Nadu State Board of Higher Secondary Education.

b. Minimum Marks by Category:
OC (Open Category): 45% average in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry
BC/MBC/DNC (Backward Class/Most Backward Class/Denotified Communities): 40% average in related subjects
SC/ST (Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe): Pass marks are sufficient

c. Attempt Limits:
Non-SC/ST candidates: Maximum 2 attempts at the qualifying examination
SC/ST candidates: Maximum 3 attempts at the qualifying examination

d. Age Limit: No age restriction for B.E./B.Tech admissions.

e. Admission Process:
Admission to B.E./B.Tech is based on TNEA (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions) scores followed by TNEA Counselling.
The TNEA 2026 counselling application window is expected to launch in the first week of May 2026.
Students are selected based on their Board Exam performance in relevant subjects.

2. B.E./B.Tech Programmes Available:
- Civil Engineering
- Mechanical Engineering
- Electrical and Electronics Engineering
- Electronics and Communication Engineering
- Computer Science and Engineering
- Information Technology
- Chemical Engineering
- Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

3. Lateral Entry (Second Year Admission):
Diploma holders in relevant engineering disciplines can apply for lateral entry into the second year of B.E./B.Tech programmes.
Selection is through TNEA lateral entry counselling.

4. M.E. (Master of Engineering) Programmes:
Eligibility is based on qualifications as per Anna University regulations.
Candidates must have a valid GATE score or TANCET score for admission.
Minimum marks and eligibility vary by specialization.

5. MBA (Master of Business Administration):
CIT Coimbatore MBA admissions are based on TANCET (Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test) scores.
Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university.
Selection process includes TANCET score, group discussion, and personal interview.

6. MCA (Master of Computer Applications):
Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree with Mathematics as one of the subjects.
Admission is through TANCET counselling.

7. M.Sc. Integrated Programmes (5 Years):
Available in Software Systems, Data Science, DCS, and AIML.
Admission is based on HSC marks and entrance test/interview.
Selection criteria include Board Exam performance, entrance test, and interview.
Application forms are available at www.cit.edu.in.

8. Ph.D. Programmes:
CIT offers Ph.D. programmes in various engineering and science disciplines.
Candidates must hold an M.E./M.Tech/M.Sc. or equivalent degree.
Selection is based on a written test and interview conducted by the department.

9. Documents Required for Admission:
- Five attested copies of TNEA allotment order (for counselling admissions)
- HSC/Diploma mark sheets (original and copies)
- Transfer Certificate from the previous institution
- Community Certificate (for reserved category students)
- Income Certificate (for fee concession/scholarship applicants)
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport-size photographs
- Migration Certificate (for students from other states/universities)
- Medical fitness certificate
- Anti-ragging affidavit (online submission at antiragging.in)

10. Important Notes:
All admissions are subject to the rules and regulations of Anna University and AICTE.
Management quota seats are available for B.E./B.Tech self-financed programmes.
NRI quota seats may be available for select programmes.
Admission to government-aided seats is strictly through TNEA/TANCET counselling.
Provisional admission may be granted pending the submission of original documents.
