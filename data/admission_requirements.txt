TITLE: Admission Requirements - Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT)
INSTITUTION: Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT), Coimbatore

================================================================================
SECTION: OVERVIEW OF ADMISSIONS
================================================================================

- Admissions at CIT are conducted based on government norms and entrance systems such as TNEA and TANCET.
- Programs include Undergraduate (UG), Postgraduate (PG), Integrated M.Sc., and Ph.D.
- Admission is subject to eligibility verification and regulatory approval.

Keywords: CIT admission overview, admission process CIT

================================================================================
SECTION: B.E. / B.TECH ADMISSION ELIGIBILITY
================================================================================

Minimum Qualification:

- Pass in HSC (Academic) with:
  - Mathematics
  - Physics
  - Chemistry

OR

- HSC Vocational Stream with engineering-related subjects

OR

- Diploma in Engineering (Civil / Mechanical / Electrical / Electronics)

OR

- Equivalent qualification approved by Tamil Nadu State Board

Age Limit:
- No age restriction

Keywords: BE eligibility CIT, BTech eligibility CIT

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION: CATEGORY-WISE MINIMUM MARKS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- OC (Open Category):
  - Minimum 45% average in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry

- BC / MBC / DNC:
  - Minimum 40% average in relevant subjects

- SC / ST:
  - Pass marks sufficient

Keywords: category marks CIT, reservation eligibility CIT

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION: ATTEMPT LIMITS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Non-SC/ST Candidates:
  - Maximum 2 attempts

- SC/ST Candidates:
  - Maximum 3 attempts

Keywords: attempt limit CIT admission

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION: ADMISSION PROCESS (B.E./B.TECH)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Admission based on TNEA (Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions)
- Selection based on:
  - Board exam performance
- Counselling conducted through TNEA

Important Timeline:
- TNEA 2026 Application expected: First week of May 2026

Keywords: TNEA CIT admission, counselling process CIT

SECTION: DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR ADMISSION
================================================================================

SECTION: IMPORTANT ADMISSION NOTES
================================================================================

- Admissions follow Anna University and AICTE regulations
- Government-aided seats:
  - Only through TNEA / TANCET counselling
- Management quota:
  - Available for self-financed B.E./B.Tech programmes
- NRI quota:
  - Available for selected programmes
- Provisional admission:
  - Allowed until document verification completion
