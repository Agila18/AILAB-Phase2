ADMISSION ELIGIBILITY:

M.Sc. Software Systems, Data Science, AI & ML:
A Pass in HSC/equivalent exam with Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry as the subjects of study. Candidates (other than SC/ST) with an aggregate of 75% and above in Mathematics, Physics, and (Chemistry or Computer Science) only need to apply.

M.Sc. Decision and Computing Sciences:
Option 1: A Pass in HSC/equivalent exam with Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry. Candidates (other than SC/ST) with 75% aggregate in Mathematics, Physics, and (Chemistry or Computer Science) only need to apply.
Option 2: A Pass in HSC/equivalent exam with one subject from Category A and 2 subjects from Category B.
CATEGORY A: Mathematics / Applied Mathematics / Statistics / Business Mathematics / Business Mathematics & Statistics.
CATEGORY B: Commerce / Economics / Accountancy / Computer Science.
Candidates (other than SC/ST) with 75% aggregate in the three subjects only need to apply.
