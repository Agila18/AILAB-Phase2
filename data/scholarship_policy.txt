DOCUMENT: CIT_SCHOLARSHIP_FINANCIAL_AID_POLICY
INSTITUTION: Coimbatore Institute of Technology
LOCATION: Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India

SECTION: OVERVIEW
- CIT facilitates more than 24 government and private scholarship schemes
- Objective: reduce financial burden for deserving students
- Covers government, central, private, alumni, and institutional aid

SECTION: GOVERNMENT_SCHOLARSHIPS_COMMUNITY_WELFARE
- Adi Dravidar Welfare Scholarship → SC/ST students
- Backward Class (BC) Scholarship → BC/MBC students
- Minority Scholarship → minority community students
- Farmer Social Security Scholarship → children of farmers

SECTION: GOVERNMENT_SCHOLARSHIPS_MERIT_REGIONAL
- Chief Minister’s Scholarship → state-level merit
- Arignar Anna Award → academic excellence
- Interstate Scholarships → Pondicherry, Andaman & Nicobar, Northeast students

SECTION: GOVERNMENT_SCHOLARSHIPS_SPECIAL_CATEGORY
- AICTE Pragathi Scholarship → women in technical education
- AICTE Saksham Scholarship → differently-abled students
- Occupational Scholarships → children of teachers, defense personnel, ex-servicemen

SECTION: FEE_CONCESSIONS
- Full tuition fee concession:
  - SC / ST students
  - Converts to Christianity
  - Applicable for government-aided courses
- Partial fee concession:
  - BC/MBC students with income below prescribed limit

SECTION: CENTRAL_GOVERNMENT_SCHEMES
- PM Special Scholarship Scheme → Jammu & Kashmir students
- Central Armed Police Forces Scholarships
- Railway Ministry Scholarship Schemes

SECTION: NATIONAL_SCHOLARSHIP_PORTAL_SCHEMES
- Central Sector Scholarship → income < Rs. 8.8 lakhs
- AICTE Pragathi → girls
- AICTE Saksham → differently-abled
- Ishan Uday Scholarship → Northeast students

SECTION: PRIVATE_AND_ALUMNI_SCHOLARSHIPS
- Tamil Nadu Educational Trust Scholarship
- ONGC Scholarship
- Siragugal Scholarship Scheme
- Alumni Trust Scholarships (1967–2017 batches)
- CIT Endowment Fund Scholarship
- Industry-sponsored scholarships (via recruiters)

SECTION: INCOME_LIMITS
- Income limits vary: Rs. 2.5 lakhs to Rs. 8.8 lakhs per annum
- Depends on scheme and government norms

SECTION: APPLICATION_PROCESS
- Submit application to Principal’s Office
- Deadline:
  - Within 30 days of reopening OR
  - Within 30 days of admission
- Must include all required supporting documents
- Late or incomplete applications → rejected

SECTION: ELIGIBILITY_CONDITIONS
- Only ONE scholarship/concession allowed at a time
- Highest benefit scheme will be awarded
- Minimum attendance: 75%
- Must maintain satisfactory academic performance

SECTION: DISCIPLINARY_REQUIREMENTS
- Scholarships based on trust and discipline
- Cancellation if:
  - Ragging involvement
  - Participation in strikes
  - Violation of college rules
- Students must maintain good conduct

SECTION: SCHOLARSHIP_RENEWAL
- Renewed annually based on eligibility
- Re-application required every year
- Performance + attendance reviewed before renewal

SECTION: SUPPORT_AND_GUIDANCE
- Principal’s Office provides guidance
- Helps match students with suitable schemes
- Updates shared via:
  - Notice boards
  - College website

SECTION: REQUIRED_DOCUMENTS
- Community / Caste Certificate
- Income Certificate
- Aadhaar Card
- Bank Account Details (student)
- Previous mark sheets
- Admission receipt & fee payment proof
- Medical certificate (if applicable)
- Scheme-specific documents (if required)
