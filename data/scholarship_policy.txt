💰 Scholarship Policy

1. Types of Scholarships:
a. Merit-based Scholarship: For top-performing students in the Dean's list.
b. Need-based Financial Aid: Designed for students from economically weaker backgrounds.
c. Sports Scholarship: Awarded to students representing the university at national or international sporting events.

2. Eligibility Criteria:
Merit scholarships require a minimum CGPA of 8.5 with no active academic backlogs.
Sports scholarships require a legitimate certification of participation and a minimum CGPA of 6.0.

3. Income Limits:
To qualify for Need-based Financial Aid, the combined annual family income must not exceed $30,000.
Tax returns or official income certificates from the government must be submitted during application.
