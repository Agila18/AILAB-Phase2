Infrastructure and Facilities - Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT), Coimbatore

1. Campus Overview:
CIT is located on a 69-acre campus on Avinashi Road, Civil Aerodrome Post, Coimbatore-641014.
The campus is approximately 2.3 km from Coimbatore International Airport.
The campus houses 14 departments, hostels, sports facilities, and various support infrastructure.

2. Laboratories:
CIT has advanced laboratories across all departments equipped with the latest instruments:
- Civil Engineering Laboratory
- Chemistry Laboratory
- Computer Laboratory
- Electrical Laboratory
- Electronics Laboratory
- Language Laboratory
- Mechanical Laboratory
- Physics Laboratory
- Structural Engineering Laboratory
- Heat Power Laboratory
- Advanced Manufacturing Technology Laboratory
- Physical Chemistry Laboratory
- Instrumentation Laboratory
- AC Machines Laboratory
- Integrated Circuits Laboratory
- VLSI Design Laboratory
- MCA Laboratory
- Communication Systems Laboratory
Each department has dedicated lab facilities for practical sessions and research.

3. Research Centres:
CIT has advanced research centres in cutting-edge fields:
- Artificial Intelligence Research Centre
- Renewable Energy Research Centre
- Materials Science Research Centre
- Big Data Analytics Research Centre
Faculty and students actively publish research papers in national and international journals and conferences.

4. Library:
The Central Library is spread across 13,881 sq. ft.
The library has approximately 1,00,000 volumes including journals, books, and research project reports.
Educational CD-ROMs and audio-video cassettes are available.
Digital access to e-books and online journals through subscriptions.
The library provides a quiet study environment with dedicated reading halls.
Library services include reference services, inter-library loan, and photocopying facilities.

5. Classrooms:
Smart classrooms equipped with LCD projectors and audio-visual aids.
Well-ventilated classrooms with adequate seating capacity.
Seminar halls for guest lectures, workshops, and presentations.
Large auditoriums for cultural programmes, convocation, and major events.

6. Computing Facilities:
Central Computing Centre with high-speed internet connectivity.
Wi-Fi coverage across the campus including hostels.
Computer labs with modern systems and licensed software.
Access to online learning platforms and digital resources.

7. E-Learning Centre:
CIT has a dedicated E-Learning Centre for digital and online education.
The centre supports blended learning approaches and virtual classroom sessions.
Students can access recorded lectures, study materials, and online courses.

8. Sports Facilities:
CIT provides extensive indoor and outdoor sports facilities:
- Cricket ground
- Basketball court
- Football ground
- Hockey field
- Tennis court
- Volleyball court
- Badminton court
- Tennikoit court
- Gymnasium and fitness centre
Students can participate in inter-college, university-level, and national-level competitions.

9. Hostel Accommodation:
3 hostels for girls and 2 hostels for boys.
Rooms with 2-sharing and 4-sharing configurations.
Each room equipped with study table, bed, almirah, and fan.
Hostel mess provides hygienic meals (breakfast, lunch, snacks, dinner).
Wi-Fi, CCTV surveillance, gymnasium, and sports facilities within hostel premises.
Hostel fees: Rs. 28,000 to Rs. 30,000 per annum.

10. Other Campus Facilities:
- 24/7 ATM service on campus
- Cafeteria and canteen serving snacks, beverages, and meals
- Stationery shop for academic supplies
- Medical/first-aid facility on campus
- CCTV surveillance across the campus for security
- Dedicated parking areas for students and staff
- Fire safety equipment installed in all buildings
- Rainwater harvesting system on campus
- Green campus initiatives with extensive tree plantation

11. Innovation and Entrepreneurship:
Innovation labs for students to work on creative projects and startups.
CIT supports students in participating in national-level hackathons and innovation challenges.
The college encourages entrepreneurial activities and provides mentorship for student startups.

12. Professional Bodies and Clubs:
Active student chapters of professional bodies:
- IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers)
- Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers
- Other discipline-specific professional bodies
20 student clubs covering arts, dramatics, Tamil, film, karate, music, photography, and sports.
Student union organizes cultural and technical events throughout the year.

13. Transport:
College bus service available for students from various parts of Coimbatore city and surrounding areas.
Dedicated parking areas for two-wheelers and four-wheelers.
The campus is well-connected by public transport.

14. Placement Infrastructure:
State-of-the-art facilities for group discussions, tests, and interviews.
Communication lab for aptitude and soft skills enhancement.
Dedicated placement library with aptitude and technical reference materials.
Interview rooms and seminar halls for pre-placement talks.
